Our senior team have extensive combined in-house regulatory expertise and regulatory recruitment specialisation. We offer our clients exceptional sector knowledge and our consulting and recruitment teams are some of the best subject matter experts in their areas of specialism.

We are well known and trusted experts in our fields of specialism. We are supported by a fantastic and diverse team of talented regulatory consultants, many having previously been Heads of Compliance, MLROs and highly regarded technical specialists in their areas of specialism. All of them have worked in regulatory-aligned roles at investment banks, private banks, brokerages, asset managers, regulators and consulting firms. Our senior team are:

James Ritchie – James is a highly experienced global markets and investment banking former compliance managing director. His regulatory career began in 1995 and he has worked in international advisory, senior management and leadership roles at Credit Suisse, Barclays Wealth and Barclays Capital. Earlier in his career he was at the Bank of England and the UK Financial Services Authority. He has a BA Economics from Nottingham University.

Malcolm Hill – Malcolm is one of The City’s longest serving, best connected and most experienced regulatory search specialists. Since 1998 his focus has been on senior-level compliance and anti-financial crime assignments at up to global head level. He established EigerSearch in 2007. Earlier in his career he was an officer in the British Army. He has an MBA and a B.Com; both from Edinburgh University.

Paul Geradine – Paul has extensive regulatory Compliance and governance experience, having held senior roles in the London Stock Exchange, UK Financial Services Authority and various investment banks, including UBS, Deutsche Bank and Credit Suisse. He began his career as a Chartered Accountant and is a graduate of Corpus Christi College, Oxford. Paul provides support to Eiger Search to help develop its profile and to enhance our regulatory consulting offering in areas in which Paul has particular expertise.
