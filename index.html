EigerSearch was formed in 2007. Our focus is the provision of combined regulatory consulting and human capital solutions. We are a London-based firm and have worked with financial services clients to provide specialist advice and human capital to minimise their regulatory risks.

Our clients leverage our regulatory technical expertise which dates from 1989 and our regulatory executive search experience from 1998.

Our target market includes global investment banks, asset managers, private banks, brokerages and consultancies. As a provider of executive search services, we have successfully placed at global, regional and local levels, numerous Heads of Compliance, Heads of Financial Crime and key senior management and team members. Our regulatory experience includes investment banking, markets, asset management and wealth management businesses.

  • We are a niche force in compliance recruitment and consulting
  • Our key differentiator is our unique combination of compliance executive search and technical compliance expertise together with an approach tailored to the specific needs of each client
  • We deliver a broad range of compliance resourcing and consulting solutions to global firms
  • In contrast to what are normally separate service providers, we are a one stop solutions business and have an alliance of partners and service providers
